Our Story

Nikhil Goswami and his sister Nisha Goswami, founders of the clothing brand GUSAI, come from a humble background. Raised in a lower-middle-class family, they had no formal training in clothing design or manufacturing. However, their father's entrepreneurial spirit rubbed off on them, and they always harbored a dream of starting their own product-based business.

Their vision was unique: a luxury streetwear brand that embodied the essence of Indian street culture. The word "streets" held a particular significance for them, as they had grown up witnessing it firsthand. GUSAI was born out of this desire to showcase Indian streetwear to the world and redefine what it meant. Their brand strives to bridge the gap between luxury and everyday wear, offering a distinct perspective on Indian street fashion.
